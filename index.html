Welcome to St. Thomas Church

Welcome to St. Thomas Church, Jalahalli, a 2000 plus members strong community, united in the spirit of the Lord. Our Church is based in North Bangalore, and our community was formed to address the spiritual needs of the Malayalee Catholics here. Coming under the Holy See at Rome, and the Archbishop of Bangalore, we follow the Liturgical celebration of the Syro Malabar Rite here.
The Syro Malabar rite has developed in unison with the rich culture of the people of Kerala, and our traditions are a reflection of the people that we are. Through our celebrations, we grow as a community, constantly in touch with our origins, as we move forward in our quest to seek and know the Almighty.

We welcome you to our midst, and may the Spirit of our Lord and Savior guide you in all your days.
